About Us

Our Mission

We want everyone to be able to achieve their biggest dream. To win a NBA championship.But since we all cant be 6'7 and shoot from 30 feet out. We wanted to make a league that the average joe can walk in and walk out feeling like a champion.We provide the gyms, the confetti and the trophies.We just want everyone to be able to say that they won something that they can be proud of.
